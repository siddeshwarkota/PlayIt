About Us

We believe in revolutionising the world of sport and unite millions for the love of the sport. Are you ready to be a part of our power-packed line-up? COMPANY PROFILETIMELINECULTURETESTIMONIAL Sportobuddy is a world that celebrates the power of sport through an offering of services, products and content. We began our journey in 2015 when a team of entrepreneurs came together to establish Sportobuddy, a Locobuddy Private Limited initiative.


We are a disruptive online portal and mobile app that integrates sports with technology to deliver a holistic sporting experience using location intelligence. Simply put, you can now book a match at your favourite turf, link up with fellow players, and be a part of sporting events based on where they are, all at the tap of a button. The portal has mapped out a universe of gyms, yoga centres, Zumba studios, adrenaline pumping adventure sports and professional coaching services. We offer a wide selection of sporting apparel, gear and accessories for aspiring athletes.


We aim to work with the existing talent pool created by local communities, across the grassroot-level, education sectors, & especially in the corporate realm - backed by some of the finest coaches and infrastructure in an organised & professional manner. At Sportobuddy, we believe in creating social communities focussing on an active lifestyle and encouraging them to step out and play, all for the love of the game.
